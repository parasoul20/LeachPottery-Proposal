Leach Pottery Website Proposal

A personalised, mobile-first, and accessibility-focused approach by Parasoul

About Parasoul

Parasoul is a small, dedicated agency offering personalised attention and flexible collaboration. We are mindful of budget while delivering high-quality, sustainable, and modern web solutions.

Project Understanding

The Leach Pottery redevelopment aims to deliver a mobile-first, accessible website integrating Shopify e-commerce, ticketing, and course bookings. The new site should showcase collections, exhibitions, and community programs while driving audience growth and reflecting the Pottery’s refreshed brand identity.

Approach & Methodology

Discovery & Planning

Understand audiences, content, and technical requirements.

Design

Mobile-first and accessibility-focused designs aligned with the refreshed brand.

Development

Shopify-based build with integrations and scalable architecture.

Testing & QA

Accessibility checks, SEO optimisation, and performance testing.

Launch & Handover

Smooth transition to the Pottery team with training and documentation.
